Welcome to Knittr!

Knittr is an app that generates custom knitting patterns. With ordinary knitting patterns, you have to select your yarn and needles to make your stitches the exact same size as the pattern designer's stitches. Otherwise, your finished knitted piece won't come out the right size. With Knittr, you can pick any gauge of needle or yarn, and Knittr will adjust the number of stitches and rows in the knitting pattern instead.
